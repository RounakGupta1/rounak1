About Us

Welcome to The Dwarka Hotel

To experience the advantage of hospitality.


One of the seven most ancient religious cities of India, Dwarka, Gujarat, is also known as the ‘Gateway to Heaven’. Thousands of devotees visit this destination to explore the birthplace of Lord Krishna who also had his kingdom here. While planning a visit to the famous Dwarkadhish Temple and Sudama Setu, one can stay at The Dwarika Hotel – Dwarka, which is situated in and around 2 km radius from the city’s prominent temples, holy shrines and railway station. The Dwarika Hotel – Dwark provides a comfortable stay at pocket-friendly prices. It also has a multi-cuisine vegetarian restaurant; Prasad – a multicuisine restaurant and a coffee shop, which serves throughout the day. With 2 banqueting spaces, it is one of those hotels in Dwarka that is best suitable to host private, social and business gatherings.
